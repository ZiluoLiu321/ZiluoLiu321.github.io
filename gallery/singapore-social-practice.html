Singapore Social Practice

2023.07

In July 2023, led by Associate Professor Liao Yuan and Counselor Zhu Yaqiang, a team of 20 undergraduate students from the College of Environmental Science and Engineering embarked on a 7-day study visit to Singapore. Over five days of visits, the team toured NUS, NTU, SMTC (Singapore Membrane Technology Center), and PUB NEWater, exploring laboratories, research centers, and campus facilities while engaging in in-depth exchanges on environmental science and engineering.
