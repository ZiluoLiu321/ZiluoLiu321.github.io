About Me

I am a doctoral student at School of Environment, Tsinghua University, specializing in traffic emissions and air quality modeling. My research uses CMAQ and other atmospheric chemistry models to investigate the sources, transformation, and transport of atmospheric pollutants.

I received my B.Eng. from the College of Environmental Science and Engineering at Nankai University (2021–2025). Before that, I attended the Foreign Language School attached to Guangxi Normal University (2015–2021).
