About Me

I am a doctoral student at Tsinghua University, specializing in traffic emissions and air quality modeling. My research uses CMAQ-ISAM to investigate the sources, transformation, and transport of air pollutants.

I received my B.Eng. from the College of Environmental Science and Engineering at Nankai University (2021–2025). Before that, I attended the Foreign Language School attached to Guangxi Normal University (2015–2021).
